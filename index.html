About V.I.S.I.O.N

V.I.S.I.O.N. simplifies eye care diagnosis using virtual reality technology. It swiftly detects eye grades, identifies conditions, and offers a user-friendly interface for enhanced efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Say hello to accurate, efficient eye care with V.I.S.I.O.N. The project led by a team of passionate Grade 12 STEM students aiming to revolutionize eye care diagnostics using virtual reality technology. Our goal is to simplify the diagnosis process and make eye care more accessible and efficient for everyone. Join us on our journey to transform the future of eye health with V.I.S.I.O.N.
